Psychology, Philosophy, Literature, and Film

The most exciting and important aspects of my work are the users that I am designing for. I take great interest in subjects like psychology and philosophy because they enable me to form a deeper understanding of the fundamental motivations that people have, and how those people determine their values. My hope is that this deeper understanding will give me a greater sense of how to appropriately use technology to enhance people's lives.

I also devote much of my time engaging with literature and film, which I believe offer additional windows into the human psyche. You can find some of my creative and analytical work on Youtube and Medium.
