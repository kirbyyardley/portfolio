About Me

The human-centric part of design is what draws me in. I get to make things for people, which means I get to learn about people.

You'll occasionally find me very amateurishly poking around inside the great minds of psychologists and philosophers like Carl Jung and Friedrich Nietzsche, or perhaps listening to whatever lectures I can find online. I'm utterly fascinated by everything that makes us human.
