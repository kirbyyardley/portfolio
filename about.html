About Me

I’m a designer based in Salt Lake City, Utah. My focus is primarily on user experience and interface design, but I also have a tons of experience writing front-end code.

Depending on the time of year you might find me hiking or skiing in one of Utah’s beautiful mountain ranges, or trapsing through the deserts in Southern Utah.

A lot of my free time is devoted to reading, writing, and creatively expressing ideas. I occasionally post things on Medium and also have a Youtube channel where I post essays about psychology, art, and film.
